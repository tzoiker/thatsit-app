What is?
Have You ever been in a situation when You saw piece of movie or heard a phrase and couldn't find out which film it is? If yes, then You are on a right way!

ThatsIt is online service in a form of mobile application that allows You to recognize movie and particular moment from an audio sample, pronounced or written phrase.

Just follow 3 simple steps:

1.Launch ThatsIt application

2.Push the button

3.Get results

ThatsIt is powered by Apache Lucene textual database and enthusiasm of students of Skolkovo Institute Of Science and Technology.
